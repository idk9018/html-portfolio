My Movie Rankings

Here are some of my top movies

1. Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is an American fantasy drama television series created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss for HBO. It is an adaptation of A Song of Ice and Fire, a series of fantasy novels by George R. R. Martin, the first of which is A Game of Thrones. The show was both produced and filmed in the United Kingdom. It premiered on HBO in the United States on April 17, 2011, and concluded on May 19, 2019, after eight seasons and 73 episodes.

2. A Man Called God

A Man Called God is a 2014 South Korean action film directed by Kim Jeong-woo and starring Song Kang-ho, Lee Na-young, and Lee Hee-joon. The film follows a man who claims to be God and his journey through the streets of Seoul.

3. Alchemy Of Souls

Alchemy of Souls is a South Korean television series starring Lee Jae-wook, Lee Se-young, Hwang Min-hyun, and Go Youn-jung. The series follows a powerful sorcerer who is trapped in the body of a woman and the challenges she faces.

4. Lincoln Lawyer

Lincoln Lawyer is a 2011 American legal thriller film directed by Brad Furman and starring Matthew McConaughey, Marisa Tomei, Ryan Phillippe, and William H. Macy. The film is based on the 2005 novel of the same name by Michael Connelly and follows a defense attorney who operates out of the back of his Lincoln Town Car.
